Privacy Policy

Routine : Diary app | Version 1.0.2
Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Routine : Diary app is developed by Amal Jose and is provided as a free, offline-first diary application. This service is intended for personal use as-is.

Summary: Routine respects your privacy completely. All your diary entries, images, and personal data are stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share any personal information. The app only connects to the internet to download optional background images and stickers from Supabase – no user data is ever transmitted.

1. Information Collection and Use

Routine does not collect any personal information. The app does not require account creation, login, email registration, or any form of user identification.

  • No personal data collection
  • No analytics tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party data sharing

2. Local Data Storage (sqflite)

All diary entries, images, stickers, and app settings are stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database via the Flutter sqflite package. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

The optional lock screen feature uses your device’s built‑in authentication (PIN, pattern, or biometric) to protect access. No credentials are stored by the app.

3. Internet Usage – Supabase Integration

Routine functions fully offline, but it can connect to the internet solely to download optional visual content:

  • Background images – a curated set is downloaded from Supabase when you choose a new background.
  • Stickers – sticker packs are downloaded from Supabase when you select them.

These downloads reduce the app’s installation size. The app only retrieves files; it never uploads any user data, device information, or analytics to Supabase or any other server. All downloaded files are stored locally on your device.

4. Permissions

The app requests only the permissions essential for its core functionality:

  • Internet – to download backgrounds and stickers (optional, can be denied).
  • Storage – to save images you add to your diary and to store downloaded backgrounds/stickers.

Routine does not access contacts, location, microphone, camera, or other sensitive device data.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on your device’s security measures (screen lock, biometric authentication, etc.). The optional lock screen adds an extra layer of protection using your device’s native authentication system – your credentials are never handled by the app.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the application. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data, including downloaded backgrounds and stickers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Routine does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. As the app does not collect any user data, it complies with Google Play's User Data and Families Policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
