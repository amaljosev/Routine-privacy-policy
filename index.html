Privacy Policy

Routine : Diary app | Version 1.0.0
Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Routine : Diary app is developed by Amal Jose and is provided as a free, offline diary application. This service is intended for personal use as-is.

Summary: Routine is a fully offline diary app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All your entries remain securely on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

Routine does not collect any personal information. The app does not require account creation, login, email registration, or any form of user identification.

  • No personal data collection
  • No analytics tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party data sharing

2. Local Data Storage (sqflite)

All diary entries and related data are stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database via the Flutter sqflite package.

Your information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Internet Usage

Routine functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection. The app does not send or receive data over the internet.

4. Permissions

The app only requests permissions strictly necessary for its core functionality (such as local storage access if required by the Android system). Routine does not access contacts, location, microphone, camera, or other sensitive device data.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on your device’s security measures (screen lock, biometric authentication, etc.). We do not have access to your diary content.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the application. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Routine does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. As the app does not collect any user data, it complies with Google Play's User Data and Families Policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the app. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
